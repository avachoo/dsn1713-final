Hyaluronic Acid SPF 40+ Essence

Neutrogena’s Hyaluronic Acid SPF 40+ Essence delivers the perfect balance of hydration and protection. Infused with hyaluronic acid, it deeply moisturizes while SPF 40+ shields your skin from harmful UV rays. Lightweight and fast-absorbing, it leaves your skin feeling smooth, plump, and radiant all day. Perfect for daily use, it’s a must-have for glowing, protected skin!

Key Ingredients

Avobenzone 3% (sunscreen) Homosalate 10% (sunscreen) Octisalate 5% (sunscreen) Octocrylene 10% (sunscreen) Inactive Ingredients: Water Glycerin Sodium Hyaluronate (Hyaluronic Acid) Dimethicone Caprylyl Glycol Butylene Glycol Phenoxyethanol Ethylhexylglycerin Xanthan Gum PEG-8 Dimethicone

Details

"Amazing product! Gave me lasting hydration throughout the dry season. Provides a natural glow and healthy skin. Would recommend to all! -Melissa Thomas"

To use Neutrogena Hyaluronic Acid SPF 40+ Essence, start with clean, dry skin. Apply a small amount evenly over your face and neck as the final step in your morning skincare routine, before makeup. Gently massage it into your skin until fully absorbed. For optimal sun protection, apply 15 minutes before sun exposure and reapply every two hours when outdoors.

Neutrogena Hyaluronic Acid SPF 40+ Essence is designed to deliver two essential benefits in one product: intense hydration and broad-spectrum sun protection. Powered by hyaluronic acid, it replenishes moisture and locks it in, giving your skin a plump and healthy appearance. The SPF 40+ shields against harmful UVA and UVB rays, making it a perfect daily essential for preventing sun damage and premature aging. Its lightweight, non-greasy texture makes it suitable for all skin types and ideal for layering under makeup or other skincare products.
